This gives you 600K+ RS3 Gold per hour and additional 400K various loot. 4. Making Cannonballs

This is a pretty straightforward method by which you can get 1M RSGP/H by Smithing Cannonballs. You’ll need the Morytania Legs 2 to smith Cannonballs and the Furnace in Port Phasmatys to be able to use this method. You simply fill your inventory with Steel Bars, and run to the Furnace to make Cannonballs.

 

5. Fishing

This is a newer method which requires 90 Fishing, and there are 3 different spots to make 800K-1.1M RSGP/H in total.

 

You should use a Fury Outfit (if you own it), Call of the Sea Aura, a Granite Lobster, a Perfect Juju Fishing Potion, and Fishing Rod-o-Matic augmented with Perks, or Crystal Rod.

 

First, fishing Blue Jellyfish gives 850K GP/H, and you should prioritize Electrified Jellies for faster catches.

 

Second, fishing at the Swarm spot is slightly more AFK, and it has a profit of 1.1M GP/H.

 

Finally, you will make about 1M/H from Sailfish which are slightly less AFK, and  you should prioritize the Swift spots for the fastest catch rate.

You can get about 2.3M RS3 Gold/H by using this method which requires 85 Smithing. You’ll need to use a Portable Forge (W84 CA), a Coal Bag, and Blacksmith’s Outfit if you own it.

 

You’ll need to make a loadout with 12 pieces of Rune, a Coal Bag, and Coal. Make sure to set the left-click option on the Forge to Smelting, and then you are ready to go. This method will allow you to AFK for around half a minute. When you are banking, you’ll be filling your Coal Bag by right-clicking on it and clicking Fill, then you press your preset key to get the rest of your ores out of the Bank.

This has a profit of 2.3M RS Gold/H. To make the most profit, you should have 75 Divination, 86 Magic, and 81 Summoning. You can additionally use a slightly different method if you have 101 Invention.

This will give you an increased rate of enriched Memories while also attracting Wisps. The Muspah Familiar is pretty much a requirement for this method as it will allow you to hold over twice the amount of Memories in your inventory.
