Who We Are

Here at Min Life we create affordable housing alternatives for those affected by economic crisis or an itch for adventure. From affordable, template tiny homes to dynamic dream vessels, we manufacturethe perfect alternative housing solution for you.
We aim to end homelessness throughout Seattle, Washington area. We believe in "housing first" beforeother solutions can provide long-term stability. Our housing solutions are primarily designed asstepping stones from homeless to home-owner. Our network includes homeless shelters and services,section 8 housing specialists, realtors, financial planners, life coaches, and more.
Our designers work with the adventurous as well on custom-built tiny homes. These hand-crafted homes provide their owners with:

  • Financial stability as a home-owner
  • Flexibility traveling short or long-distance
  • Potential for passive rental income
  • Clarity of mind without the clutter of life

If you're looking for long-term financial stability or a rekindled spirit of adventure, give us a call to see what opportunities await you through a minimalist lifestyle.
