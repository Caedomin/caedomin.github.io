Applying the Scriptures to Ourselves

Applying the scriptures to our own lives can deepen our understanding of the gospel and strengthen our testimony on the principles taught. Nephi wrote, "I did liken all scriptures unto us, that it might be for our profit and learning." (1 Nephi 19:23). There is much we can learn from the same scripture when applied throughout our lives as our circumstances change and our testimony develops.
